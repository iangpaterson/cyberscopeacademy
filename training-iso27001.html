ISO 27001 PATHWAY

Build Your Career in Cyber Security Management with ISO 27001

ISO/IEC 27001:2013 is acknowledged as the de facto global information security management standard and is used by organisations of any size to ensure the protection of their information assets and achieve a significant competitive advantage. The importance of cyber security has created an unprecedented demand for qualified information security managers in companies throughout the UK.

ISO 27001 qualifications are the key to building a successful career in information security management – cybersecurity jobs in the UK requiring ISO 27001 qualifications start at £40K/year for junior management positions rising to £102K/year for senior roles.

Develop Knowledge and Skills

The IT Governance ISO 27001 learning pathway provides training courses from Foundation through to Advanced level. All courses are delivered by experienced practitioners who will equip you with the knowledge and skills required to plan, implement, maintain and audit an ISO 27001-compliant Information Security Management System (ISMS).

Gain Industry-Recognised Qualifications

All of the courses offer the opportunity to achieve ISO 17024-accredited qualifications awarded by the International Board for IT Governance Qualifications (IBITGQ). The ISO 27001 IBITGQ qualifications (CIS F, CIS LI, CIS LA) are industry-recognised and highly regarded by employers worldwide.

About Cyberscope TV

Cyberscope TV is a crowd sharing platform for opportunities, challenges and technologies changing the world of Cyber Security.

We have secured our on screen comments and chat using an RSA algorithm. Video and audio chats use WebRTC technology which in its turn makes use of DTLS protocol by default. Datagram Transport Layer Security (DTLS) is used to provide communications privacy for datagram protocols. This fosters a secure signaling channel that cannot be tampered with. In other words, no eavesdropping or message forgery can occur on a DTLS encrypted connection.

As regards our storage and streaming, we use OAuth plus our additional authenticated and encrypted connection channels to deliver data only to authorised users.
